Teaching Online: my journey with udemy platform

15 December, 2020 | Mohamed Nid Said | Teaching

Hello, I'm Mohamed and in this blog I'm going to share with you my journey with udemy platform,For those of you who don’t know what udemy is read this part :

Udemy, founded in May 2010, is an online learning platform aimed at professional adults and students. As of Jan 2020, the platform has more than 50 million students and 57,000 instructors teaching courses in over 65 languages. There have been over 295 million course enrollments.

I started learning from udemy in 2019, I was so interested in the internet and I heard about this platform from youtube. I was bad at it because my english was not that impressive but I did what I could. .

I still remember my first online course, it was about website creation. I needed a website but I didn't know how to get one so I went to udemy to learn. The best thing about udemy is that you can get free courses at the right time, you just need to be on time and know what you are looking for.

After I was familiar with udemy I wanted to be an instructor and teach what I've learned so I did my best and I recorded my first course WordPress For Beginners : Free domain name and hosting.

The first month was very disappointing because I didn't get much interested students in my course but with time people started to notice and now I have 3,915 students and a 3.7 rating.

This was the start for me and i was so excited about it, so i made more courses :

  • WordPress For Beginners : Free domain name and hosting.
  • The Complete Shopify Making Money Selling E-books Course.
  • Blogger for beginners : unlimited Organic Free Traffic
  • WordPress for Beginners: Create unlimited Websites for Free.
  • Pinterest Marketing: The Ultimate Guide to Success 2020.
