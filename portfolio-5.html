Social Media Marketing Optimization

Learn a three-step recipe for optimizing your social media marketing mix. Get a fresh perspective on your content, your choice of platform, and your strategy. Join digital communication strategist Martin Waxman for this illuminating short course that will get even the most experienced marketers out of a social media rut. Learn how to analyze what's working—and what's not—with SWOT analysis, and adapt your plan to capitalize on your strengths.

Learning Objectives:

  • Establishing your social media strategy
  • Conducting a social media audit
  • Analyzing your SWOT results
  • Unique drivers for Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and more
  • Refining your content mix
